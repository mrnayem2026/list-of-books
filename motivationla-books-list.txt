Here's a list of notable motivational books that can inspire and drive you to achieve your goals:

1. **"You Are a Badass: How to Stop Doubting Your Greatness and Start Living an Awesome Life" by Jen Sincero**
   - A motivational guide to overcoming self-doubt and achieving personal success.

2. **"The Power of Positive Thinking" by Norman Vincent Peale**
   - Explores the benefits of positive thinking for achieving a fulfilling life.

3. **"The 5 Second Rule: Transform your Life, Work, and Confidence with Everyday Courage" by Mel Robbins**
   - Introduces a simple tool for overcoming hesitation and taking action.

4. **"Awaken the Giant Within: How to Take Immediate Control of Your Mental, Emotional, Physical and Financial Destiny!" by Tony Robbins**
    - Offers strategies for taking control of your life and achieving your dreams.

5. **"Can't Hurt Me: Master Your Mind and Defy the Odds" by David Goggins**
   - A memoir and self-help book focusing on mental toughness and overcoming obstacles.

6. **"The Magic of Thinking Big" by David J. Schwartz**
   - Encourages readers to set high goals and think big to achieve success.
