Sure! Here's a list of popular and highly recommended business books:

1. **"Good to Great: Why Some Companies Make the Leap... and Others Don't" by Jim Collins**
2. **"The Lean Startup: How Today's Entrepreneurs Use Continuous Innovation to Create Radically Successful Businesses" by Eric Ries**
3. **"Thinking, Fast and Slow" by Daniel Kahneman**
4. **"Start with Why: How Great Leaders Inspire Everyone to Take Action" by Simon Sinek**
5. **"The Innovator's Dilemma: When New Technologies Cause Great Firms to Fail" by Clayton M. Christensen**
6. **"Zero to One: Notes on Startups, or How to Build the Future" by Peter Thiel with Blake Masters**
7. **"The E-Myth Revisited: Why Most Small Businesses Don't Work and What to Do About It" by Michael E. Gerber**
8. **"Drive: The Surprising Truth About What Motivates Us" by Daniel H. Pink**
9. **"Lean In: Women, Work, and the Will to Lead" by Sheryl Sandberg**
10. **"The 7 Habits of Highly Effective People: Powerful Lessons in Personal Change" by Stephen R. Covey**
11. **"Blue Ocean Strategy: How to Create Uncontested Market Space and Make the Competition Irrelevant" by W. Chan Kim and Renée Mauborgne**
12. **"Shoe Dog: A Memoir by the Creator of Nike" by Phil Knight**
13. **"Principles: Life and Work" by Ray Dalio**
14. **"Measure What Matters: How Google, Bono, and the Gates Foundation Rock the World with OKRs" by John Doerr**
15. **"Built to Last: Successful Habits of Visionary Companies" by Jim Collins and Jerry I. Porras**
16. **"The Hard Thing About Hard Things: Building a Business When There Are No Easy Answers" by Ben Horowitz**
17. **"Rework" by Jason Fried and David Heinemeier Hansson**
18. **"Crushing It!: How Great Entrepreneurs Build Their Business and Influence—and How You Can, Too" by Gary Vaynerchuk**
19. **"The One Minute Manager" by Kenneth H. Blanchard and Spencer Johnson**
20. **"Purple Cow: Transform Your Business by Being Remarkable" by Seth Godin**

These books cover a range of topics including leadership, innovation, entrepreneurship, management, and personal development.
